How we’ve helped: CredAbility
Size of Portfolio

41,772 sq ft

Client Objectives

CredAbility, which had doubled in size to approximately 550 employees over the past three years, provides confidential counselling and education services to more than 630,000 people annually in all 50 states, making data security a critical need. The organisation had outgrown its headquarters in the United Way building at the edge of downtown Atlanta and desired closer access to MARTA (rapid transit) for its employees.

End Result

While researching available locations in the downtown submarket, SavillsCresa targeted the 270 Peachtree Street building, which was formerly the headquarters for Georgia Power Company. The building has an enhanced power source and high-level security for its on-site data centre, both of which fulfilled CredAbility’s requirements for its 24/7 call centre operation. In addition, with three adjacent vacant floors, CredAbility could easily expand by 30% into 40,000 sq ft of office space.
