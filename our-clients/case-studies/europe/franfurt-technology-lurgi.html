How we’ve helped: Lurgi GmbH/Air Liquide Engineering & Construction
Job Requirement

The integration of Lurgi GmbH into the Air Liquide Group required more efficient spatial structures, with the request for a modern company headquarters. As part of this requirement the approximate 46,000 sq m of office space currently occupied was to be reduced to approximately 25,000 sq m. The three scenarios to examine were: optimise current space, analyse property development and rent an existing property.

Implementation

We analysed the real estate market in order to identify suitable developments/properties in accordance with Lurgi‘s requirement profile, compiled a spatial and functional programme taking into account individual business units, looked at optimising spatial structures in an existing property, and compared the top properties/developments vs remaining in the current property.

End Result

The office space was reduced to approximately 25,000 sq m, with a rental reduction in accordance with the savings target. Internal communication links were improved, and the lease terms allowed for dynamic growth through first right of refusal and an option to surrender part of the space.
