How we’ve helped: NBC Universal
Job Requirement

Advise and help NBC to decide the best location option for them before ending its lease contract

Size of Portfolio

2,800 sq m

Client Objective

Save costs (its contract was signed in 2007 in the highest market moment of the last years)

Challenges Faced

Realise an exhaustive analysis of the real needs of space that our client had at this moment; search for alternatives that could fit with the corporate image of the company; study the cost of different options.

End Result

Was decided that the best option was to eliminate 500 sq m of the lease contract and to renegotiate with the property a new contract of 2,300 sq m in the same building they were. Pº de la Castellana 95–Torre Europa (Prime Area).
