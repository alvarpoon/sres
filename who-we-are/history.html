Founded.
Savills becomes a limited company and obtains full listing on the London Stock Exchange becoming Savills plc.
Savills consolidates its growing presence in Asia, joining forces with First Pacific Davies and is rebranded FPD Savills.
Services, including Lease Administration, Location Planning, and Capital Markets are now offered. Savills plc moves into FTSE 250 London Stock Exchange Listing. Same year, it acquires First Pacific Davies.
FPD Savills global is rebranded to Savills.
Savills announced the proposed acquisition of Studley, one of the leading independent commercial agency businesses in the US, which together with our existing US business will trade as Savills Studley.
Savills is a global real estate services provider listed on the London Stock Exchange. We have an international network of over 600 offices and associates and over 27,000 staff throughout the Americas, the UK, Continental Europe, Asia Pacific, Africa and the Middle East, offering a broad range of specialist advisory, management and transactional services to clients all over the world.
