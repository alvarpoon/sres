“The search for high-quality is increasingly challenging for companies. Many of them are forced to extend their leases.”
Marcus Mornhart, Head of Office Agency Germany
  • Companies with requirements for large contiguous space in good locations are faced with a particularly acute shortage of options. Indeed, lettings of such space are now almost only possible in new developments and these remain scarce.This situation is reflected accordingly in rental growth with prime rents rising in almost all major cities in Germany.
  • In terms of take-up, 2013 was a relatively weak year for the Frankfurt office market. Deals totalled approximately 439,000 sq m; almost 14% below the previous year's level and slightly short of the average figure for the last five years (approx. 451,000 sq m). This decline in take-up was primarily attributable to the low number of major lettings.
