Supply, net take-up and vacancy rate, 2005–Q2/2014

Grade A office rental indices, Q1/2005–Q2/2014

  • After consecutive declines over the past four quarters, rents rose 1.1% QoQ to RMB314.7 per sq m per month by the end of Q2/2014.
  • Net take-up jumped to 87,000 sq m from 54,000 sq m in the previous quarter, the highest level over the past eight quarters, and accumulated to 141,000 sq m in the first half of 2014, nearly 2.5 times that of FY2013.
  • Six projects are scheduled for handover in the second half of 2014, offering a total leasable office GFA of 428,000 sq m to the market. City-wide vacancy rates, however, are expected to rise to around 5%. Overall rents are predicted to remain stable.
