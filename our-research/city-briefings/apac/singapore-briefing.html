“2013 was a year of recovery for the office leasing market, which recorded improving rents and higher occupancy rates.”
  • Real economy sectors, such as resources, oil and gas, and professional services companies, continued to account for the largest share of office take-up.
  • The overall vacancy rate of CBD Grade A offices tracked by Savills declined for the fourth consecutive quarter, from 7.8% at the end of 2012 to 3.4% by the end of Q4/2013.
  • Savills expects the recovery phase to continue over the coming quarters, with moderate rental growth in CBD Grade A offices.
