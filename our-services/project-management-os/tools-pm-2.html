A secure web-based system for managing construction project documentation throughout the design and construction phases of the project. It enables companies to share, track, search and archive information (such as building plans, emails and faxes) through one secure website.

Benefits include:

  • Reduces risks to project delivery with time, cost and quality management;
  • Increases efficiency of project management resources; and
  • Can be accessed anywhere there is an internet connection.
